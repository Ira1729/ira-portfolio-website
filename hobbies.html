Reading/Writing

I am an avid reader, I like science fiction, adventure and biographies. Few of my favourite reads are Famous Five by Enid Bython (22 Books), Harry Potter by J K Rowling, Wings of Fire by APJ Abdul Kalam and Elon Musk by Issac Walterson.

The books I read give me a glimpse into the minds of people who lived! It's one of those pleasant ways through which I can express the world as I understand it!

I started writing at the age of 9, since then most of my poems are inspired by common occurrences and themes in my life, are they special? Ofcourse every day is!, "To make something special you just have to believe it's special" -- Master Oogway

I have published 25 of my poems under title "Timestamps" in Writer's Pocket publication, I mostly prefer rhythmic style but recently trying to explore freestyle too.

"Poetry is when emotion has found its thought and thought has found words" - Robert Frost

Juggling Number Theory

In 9th grade, while reading polynomial functions I was introduced to pascal's triangle, I was fascinated by the beautiful combination of mathematics and patterns.

Soon after I made a pattern which I named 'Number Matrix' :D, I used to work on that pattern, form equations to explain it, then generalize those equations. I made the general formulas for AP and GP, later studying it in grade 10.

Soon after I was interested in finding an equation for perimeter of ellipse, knowing elliptic integrals can't be solved, and so we have approximations, I set out on a experimental approach to come up with the empirical formula pi(a+b) where a and b are major axis respectively.

Reimann Hypothesis, Collatz conjecture etc grabbed my interest, so often at times I like to work over these problems.
