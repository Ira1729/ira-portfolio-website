Atharva Aalok

I am a final year undergraduate at the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. I am excited by the research in robotics, automation and design especially geared towards aerial systems.

Currently, I am working with Prof. R.I. Sujith and Dr. Induja Pavithran at IIT Madras, on the different forms of tipping and their prediction using Early Warning Signals (EWSs). At the moment, I am focussing on rate-delayed and rate-induced tipping and on quantifying the reliability of EWSs in these tipping regimes.

During summer 2022, I worked as an intern with Prof. Suresh Sundaram at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore. We are working on designing a jet based UAV with Short Takeoff and Landing (STOL) capabilities that can operate at high altitudes (5000m+).

I am also interning at The ePlane Company, where I am working on automating India's first air-taxi.


NOTE: I am applying for graduate school for the Fall 2023 admit.
